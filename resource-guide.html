Emerald City Resource Guide

Your comprehensive guide to services and resources in Seattle

The Emerald City Resource Guide puts the power of change in people's hands. This portable, pocket-sized guidebook connects people to services like meals, shelters, healthcare facilities, harm reduction services and more.

Access the online Resource Guide database here.

Every year 50,000 copies of this guide are distributed through a network of over 200 partners — including, but not limited to, a peer network of vendors, service providers, mutual aid groups, metro bus drivers and emergency first responders. As more agencies move their information online, this printed guide is available for people who do not have reliable access to technology to connect to supportive service.
