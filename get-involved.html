Get Involved

Join our community and help create positive change

Real Change takes all of us.

The community of readers, volunteers, journalists, staff, and contributors work together to build cross-class change.

There is a role for all of us in this work, and we invite you to join the movement for our shared vision of the future: a world where all people are treated with dignity and respect, regardless of housing status.

Support Our Vendors

Real Change readers put more than one million dollars annually directly into the pockets of our vendors, but that's not all. Vendors say the relationships that get forged everyday on the street matter to them even more. Find a vendor near you.

Become a Supporter

Real Change relies on grassroots, individual donors for roughly 60% of our revenue. Your gift powers the weekly newspaper, community mobilization for advocacy, and vendor opportunity. Join the movement and be a part of a solution today.

Be a Real Change Volunteer

Many critical roles within Real Change are supported by more than 300 volunteers who serve thousands of hours each year. We can help match your interests and availability to a wide variety of important volunteer opportunities.

Take Action

Real Change regularly asks supporters to take action on issues of importance to our vendors and community allies. Sign up for email action alerts for opportunities to get involved.

Internships and Employment

Real Change offers a variety of employment opportunities to qualified applicants. Maybe there is a job here for you.
